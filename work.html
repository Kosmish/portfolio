 Flash Game: FBLA RPG

ActionScript 2.0

Flash

Illustrator

This is the game that I worked on for the Future Business Leaders of America (FBLA) competition during my senior year of high school in 2012. The topic was to create any type of game that tests users on their knowledge of basic Economics while rewarding players for answering correctly. FBLA RPG is a business simulator where you compete against 3 AI's to expand your brand across a city. The business who has the most reveune by the end of 30 days is victorious.
